# Guide: List of Abbreviations

## What this file is about

This file is the glossary for repeated technical terms in the thesis. Its job is not to be impressive or comprehensive. Its job is to make the thesis easy to read.

## What to focus on

- Every abbreviation listed here should appear in the thesis body.
- Every important repeated abbreviation in the thesis body should appear here.
- The long form should match the wording used in the chapters.
- The list should support the thesis topic directly: UAV, 5G, cloud, identity, encryption, standards, privacy, and threat modeling.

## What to watch for

- UAS and UAV used inconsistently.
- Same idea written in two different ways.
- Abbreviations included but never used.
- Important repeated abbreviations missing from the list.
- Capitalization and naming not matching the chapter text.

## Quick scan categories

### UAV and mission terms

Check terms such as UAV, UAS, BVLOS, GNSS, RTH, Dock, payload, and mission language for consistency.

### 5G and network terms

Check terms such as 3GPP, gNB, RAN, PDU, PDCP, QoS, MNO, VPN, IPsec, and IKEv2.

### Cloud and application terms

Check terms such as ACS, MQTT, JWT, RBAC, PaaS, VNet, WAF, and the Microsoft identity platform.

### Security and privacy terms

Check terms such as CIA, AAA, MFA, SBOM, DPIA, PII, STRIDE, and zero-trust-related terminology.
