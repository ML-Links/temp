# Better Prompt For This Task

## Why the earlier request was misunderstood

- I interpreted guide as a reviewer's reading checklist or evaluation aid.
- Your real goal was different: you wanted a detailed reading companion that retells the thesis content itself in simpler language.
- Phrases like guide, parallel reading, and main things to consider can still sound like a checking template unless the prompt explicitly says explain the actual thesis content section by section.
- The missing instruction was that the guide should let you understand most of the chapter without reopening the thesis repeatedly.

## What your prompt should have said

Use this prompt when you want the same result again:

Rewrite all guide files in the phase_3_writing guide folder for Sharaf Kamel.

Do not write reviewer checklists, evaluation criteria, or high-level chapter maps.

For each chapter, write a detailed reading companion in simple words and bullet style.
The guide must explain the actual content of the thesis section by section and subsection by subsection, almost like a compressed retelling of the chapter.

I want to be able to read the guide and understand the thesis itself, not only what I should check.

For every section and subsection:

- explain what the student is saying
- restate the technical point in simpler language
- say what the reader should understand from that part
- keep the real structure and order of the thesis
- keep it detailed and concrete, not abstract

Do not:

- write only this section is about
- write only focus on or watch for checklists
- stay at high level
- turn it into feedback to the student

Write it as if you are summarizing the thesis for someone who is reading in parallel and wants the full content in easier bullets.

Update the files directly in the guide folder.

## Short version

If you want a shorter instruction, this is enough:

Rewrite the guides as detailed plain-language summaries of the thesis content itself, section by section, not as review checklists. I want to understand the chapter from the guide alone.
